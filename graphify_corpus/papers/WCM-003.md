## Overview
Extends minimal-cell whole-cell simulation to show that cell-scale behaviors can emerge from integrated subcellular models in JCVI-syn3A.

## Abstract
Extends minimal-cell whole-cell simulation to show that cell-scale behaviors can emerge from integrated subcellular models in JCVI-syn3A.

## Methods Summary
Combined a living minimal-cell simulation with experimental benchmarking to test emergent behaviors across growth and gene-expression processes.

## Contribution
Demonstrated that integrated minimal-cell models can recover nontrivial emergent behaviors rather than only isolated subsystem outputs.

## Limitations
- Still limited by coarse-grained spatial assumptions and incomplete measurements for several molecular processes
