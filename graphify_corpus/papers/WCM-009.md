- Journal: Biophysical Journal
- DOI: 10.1016/j.bpj.2015.07.030
- Method class: Mechanistic models
- Organism: Escherichia coli
- PDF status: landing_page_only
- Modeled objects: Whole-cell integration, Gene-expression machinery, Modeling framework

## Overview
Builds a kinetic model of small-subunit ribosome assembly as a tractable subsystem on the path toward whole-cell ribosome biogenesis modeling.

## Abstract
Builds a kinetic model of small-subunit ribosome assembly as a tractable subsystem on the path toward whole-cell ribosome biogenesis modeling.

## Methods Summary
Constructed a kinetic assembly model constrained by biogenesis intermediates and experimental timing information.

## Contribution
Breaks a major macromolecular process into a form that can be plugged into larger whole-cell simulations.

## Limitations
- Covers one subsystem and therefore cannot explain cell-wide coupling without additional modules
